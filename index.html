Bout Me

///1000101///090//224325

Look, I'll be honest. You're probably not going to read this whole thing. So here's the short version.

I've been doing this for almost a decade. I started in 2016 and over the years I've done a bit of everything. Graphic Design, Video Editing, 3D modeling, Performance Marketing, Social Media, Videography, Brand Management and Production.

I've worked on brands like Top Ramen, Cup Noodles, FILA, Puravankara, and many more. That means I understand how brands actually work in the real world, not just on decks and brainstorm boards.

I've built campaigns, scaled social pages, led teams, cracked launches, fixed broken brand voices, and shipped work under impossible timelines. Sometimes all at once.

I don't believe in chasing trends for the sake of it. I believe in clarity, craft, and doing work that earns attention.

There's more to the story. But this should tell you enough to know whether we should talk.
